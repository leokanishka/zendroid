Why this works:

The human brain is optimized to recognize vibrant colors (like the red of a ripe fruit) as high-value rewards. Major apps use these specific HSL values to trigger a subconscious "search-and-reward" loop. By converting these into thin-line monochrome glyphs, we shift the cognitive load from the **Emotional Brain** (visual craving) to the **Analytical Brain** (reading the label).
